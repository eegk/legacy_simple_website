Background

Post Doctoral Researcher at Universidade Federal de Santa Catarina since 2016. Ph.D. in Bioinformatics from Universidade Federal de Minas Gerais (2012-2016). Bachelor Degree in Biological Sciences from Universidad Nacional Mayor de San Marcos (2011). Stronghold in Bioinformatics and data analysis with passion about Immunology.

Motivation

My main motivator is the existence of patterns within apparently complex systems. From the simplest organisms such as plasmids to structured communities of diverse molecular types of machinery working together. For that reason, my research purpose is currently to identify, understand and describe those patterns within different biological contexts. Immunology, Cancer, Virome and all the Omics.
